FAQs

Common Questions About Our Cleaning Services

Answers to common questions about professional cleaning services in Nairobi. Can't find yours? Get in touch directly.

Call 0709 004 600 WhatsApp Us
What areas in Nairobi do you serve?

We serve all areas in Nairobi including Westlands, Kilimani, Karen, Langata, Kileleshwa, Lavington, and surrounding neighbourhoods. Contact us to confirm availability at your specific location.

How much do your cleaning services cost?

Pricing depends on the service type, the size of the space, and cleaning frequency. Rates start from KSh 2,500 for basic house cleaning. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you supply your own cleaning equipment and products?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies, equipment, and eco-friendly products. If you have specific products you prefer, we are happy to use them instead.

Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?

Absolutely. All our cleaners undergo thorough background checks and are fully insured. We prioritise your safety and security, so you can trust our team in your home or office.

How do I schedule a cleaning service?

Call us on 0709 004 600, send a WhatsApp message, or fill in the contact form on this page. We will discuss your needs and arrange a convenient time.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. For recurring services, you may pause or cancel at any time with proper notice. We are flexible and understanding of changing schedules.

Do you offer one-time deep cleaning services?

Yes. We specialise in one-time deep cleaning for move-in/move-out situations, post-construction cleaning, spring cleaning, or any occasion requiring intensive cleaning.

Can I book cleaning services on weekends?

Yes, we work seven days a week including weekends. Weekend bookings may have limited availability, so we recommend scheduling in advance.

How long does a typical house clean take?

A standard 2–3 bedroom apartment typically takes 2–4 hours. The duration depends on the size of your home and the services requested. We will provide a time estimate when you book.

What hygiene and safety measures do your cleaners follow?

Our cleaners wear gloves and masks, use health-authority-approved disinfectants, and follow all recommended hygiene protocols to ensure your safety and theirs at all times.
