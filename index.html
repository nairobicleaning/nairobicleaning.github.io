Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about cleaning services in Nairobi

What areas in Nairobi do you serve?

We serve all areas in Nairobi including Westlands, Kilimani, Karen, Langata, Kileleshwa, Lavington, and surrounding neighborhoods. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.

How much do your cleaning services cost?

Our pricing depends on the type of service, size of the space, and frequency of cleaning. We offer competitive rates starting from KES 2,500 for basic house cleaning. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you provide your own cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies, equipment, and eco-friendly cleaning products. However, if you have specific products you prefer us to use, we're happy to accommodate your request.

Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?

Absolutely. All our cleaners undergo thorough background checks and are fully insured. We prioritize your safety and security, so you can trust our team in your home or office.

How do I schedule a cleaning service?

You can schedule a cleaning service by calling us at 0709 004 600, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form on our website. We'll discuss your needs and arrange a convenient time.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. For recurring services, you can pause or cancel at any time with proper notice. We're flexible and understanding of changing schedules.

Do you offer one-time deep cleaning services?

Yes, we specialize in one-time deep cleaning services for move-in/move-out situations, post-construction cleaning, spring cleaning, or any occasion that requires intensive cleaning.

Can I get a cleaning service on weekends?

Yes, we offer cleaning services 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays. Weekend bookings may have limited availability, so we recommend scheduling in advance.

How long does a typical house cleaning take?

The duration depends on the size of your home and the services requested. A standard 2-3 bedroom apartment typically takes 2-4 hours. We'll provide a time estimate when you book.

What COVID-19 safety measures do you have in place?

Our cleaners wear masks and gloves, use disinfectants approved by health authorities, maintain social distancing, and follow all recommended safety protocols to ensure your safety and theirs.
