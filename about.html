Our Story

Who We Are

Best Cleaning Nairobi — trading as BestCare Cleaning — was founded with a single purpose: to bring honest, reliable, and thorough cleaning services to homes and businesses across Nairobi. What started as a small team based in Westlands has grown into one of the city's most trusted cleaning companies, serving thousands of clients across more than 30 neighbourhoods.

"We don't just clean spaces — we restore the feeling of home and the confidence of a professional environment."

Our journey has been shaped by the simple belief that quality cleaning should be accessible, consistent, and free of hassle. Every member of our team is personally vetted, trained to our standards, and equipped with professional-grade, eco-friendly products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment.

From one-off deep cleans and post-construction clears to ongoing domestic and commercial contracts, we tailor every service to the specific needs of each client. We are proud to be the cleaning partner of choice for homeowners, landlords, property managers, and businesses throughout Nairobi.
