Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

How much does house cleaning cost in Nairobi?
Regular house cleaning in Nairobi starts at KSh 4,200 for a 1-bedroom apartment. A 2-bedroom apartment is typically KSh 5,200–6,500, and a 3–4 bedroom house ranges from KSh 7,000–9,500. Exact prices depend on the size, condition and specific requirements of the property. Contact us for a free, written quote.
How much does deep cleaning cost in Nairobi?
Deep cleaning starts at KSh 9,500 for a 1-bedroom apartment, KSh 12,000–14,500 for a 2-bedroom, and KSh 16,500–22,000 for a 3–4 bedroom home. Deep cleaning covers everything in a regular clean, plus inside cabinets and appliances, grout scrubbing, baseboards and high-reach areas.
Are there any hidden fees?
No, never. All our prices are fully inclusive — there are no hidden fees or surprise charges. The only addition that may apply is a travel supplement of KSh 1,000–2,500 for properties outside central Nairobi, and this will always be clearly stated in your written quote before you confirm a booking.
Do you offer discounts for regular bookings?
Yes. We offer a 10% discount on regular cleaning contracts of 4 or more visits per month. Monthly and bi-weekly domestic contracts also receive preferential rates. Commercial contracts of 12 months or longer may qualify for up to 15% off. Ask us about our recurring booking packages when requesting a quote.
What is included in the price?
All our prices include the cleaning team, all professional-grade eco-friendly cleaning products, all equipment (mops, vacuums, microfibre cloths, etc.) and VAT. You do not need to provide any materials. The only add-on is the travel supplement for remote areas, which is always disclosed upfront.
How do I get an exact quote?
The quickest way is to call us on 0709 004 600 or WhatsApp us with your property size, location and the service you need. We aim to respond with a written quote within one hour during business hours. You can also fill in our online contact form and we will get back to you promptly.
