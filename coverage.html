Where We Operate

Professional Cleaning, City-Wide

Best Cleaning Nairobi — trading as BestCare Cleaning — operates across the full extent of Nairobi County, from the high-end gated communities in the north to the established residential estates to the east, west, and south of the city. Since 2015, we have built a city-wide network of trained cleaning professionals who operate locally in each zone, enabling fast response times and consistent service quality wherever you are.

"We bring the same professional standard to every estate — whether you're in Runda or Rongai, Muthaiga or Umoja."

Our coverage spans the five broad zones of Nairobi County: the upmarket northern suburbs, the affluent western corridors, Nairobi's central business and diplomatic districts, the established eastern and south-eastern residential neighbourhoods, and the rapidly growing southern outskirts including Rongai and Ngong. We operate six days a week and offer same-day and emergency cleaning services across all zones on request.

If you do not see your estate listed below, do not hesitate to contact us — we are expanding our coverage continuously and may still be able to accommodate your location. Call or WhatsApp us on 0709 004 600 to confirm availability in your area.
